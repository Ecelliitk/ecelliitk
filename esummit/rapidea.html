Rapidea /fun

Rapidea (Rapid-Idea) very simplistically simulates an entrepreneur’s journey for those who aren’t entrepreneurs. It is a 2-hour compressed version of several such competitions which are spread over the weekend. So, if you want to take a closer look at running a business/ start-up but not want to commit an entire weekend to this, Rapidea is the solution.



How it Works

Rapidea has been structured to keep it as intuitive as possible. This lets Rapidea be a game for all rubbing off the need of any pre-requisites. It takes you through several rounds,enthusiastically educating entrepreneurship all the way. To abide by the rules of any game, a winner shall emerge at the end of all rounds (taken cumulatively)

Round 1: Find the Best Collaborators

This round is about finding the best business partners and allies, in other words forming a team. Don’t worry and run around finding teammates for participating. Right there, this round shall help you form a team an entrepreneur’s way! You need to compete from the very beginning of the journey.


Round 2: Innovate and Solve

Each team shall be given a problem statement and you need to come up with an innovative solution - ‘real and existing’ or ‘imaginary and hypothetical’. Just manage to come up with a brief plan on how your solution is the best way to solve the problem and how would you go about implementing it (taking economic viability, sustainability etc. shall earn you more points) Round 3: Advertise your Creation

Here comes the round, when you finally gear up and reach out to your potential customers. Keep in mind the rules of media and publicity, creativity and catchiness to be successful in delivering what you created. Use papers, acting skills, music, or anything at all to capture the imaginations of the users and come up with an advertisement to convince users with the viability of your solution. Kudos! You will have just been through ‘three’ primary stages of any entrepreneur from starting with just the enthusiasm! Getting ready to begin from an Idea to Execution. All set and steady to come up with a Business Plan for the solution Energized to startup and pitch to get Your First Few Customers Rapidea lets you practise all the tactics and tools you will learn in our workshops! Do not miss!
