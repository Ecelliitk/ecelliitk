WHAT'S E-SUMMIT ABOUT / And how its been designed for you

E-Cell, IIT Kanpur presents the third edition of eSummit, titled ‘Ready, Steady, Start-Up!’. This edition stands out from all others, as we focus on start-ups in tier-2 cities like Kanpur, Lucknow, Indore etc. and work to build an environment beyond what any other Entrepreneurship Summit does. So along with workshops, talks and competitions, this 3-day Summit will play host to a lot more - see our full article.

Upstart

An event that caters to the startups of Kanpur and neighbouring cities, Upstart - Our highlight - stands apartfrom the conventional Start-up Competition and 10-minute pitching; we strive to give our participants more than just the prize. The start-ups go through multiple rounds of screening, which are essentially brainstorming sessions at different levels. Finalists get to develop their ideas and hone their business plans with a panel of mentors.
More Details

Pitch Ur Product

Pitch Ur Product is organized with the aim to provide participants with an opportunity to present their innovation or innovative idea (e.g. summer project or any other such research work) wrapped up as a potential working startup in future.This event examines the ability of the participants to evaluate the market, the customer needs and their distribution strategy for a product they have already prototyped.

Socha

SoCha, short for Social Challenge, is a competition aimed at directing entrepreneurial minds towards the social problems that plague society today, and encourage them to provide a viable and sustainable solution for it. It is not just about the profits, but rather about the social impact their idea can have.

Decrypt

Decrypt is the case study competition wherein you are given a start-up case/crisis and you step into the shoes of a founding member to present and defend your solution.

Networking Session

Provides a platform for Startups to showcase their ideas among hundreds of potential users and investors, with a realistic possibility of product acquisition It also serves as an excellent networking platform. Rapidea

Rapidea is a fun event designed to test the problem solving skills of an individual in a limited time frame- an essential attribute for any aspiring entrepreneur. The participant will be faced with a real life problem and he/she is expected to come up with a feasible solution for it.

FAQS / find your answers

  • Is it all free?
  • How to reach IIT Kanpur?
  • What to do when I reach the campus?
  • Where would I stay?
Nope. The online registration is not chargeable but you'll need to pay ₹800 as basic food and accommodation charges, upon your arrival here. Beyond that, there are no specific event charges for any of our events or talks (including Workshops).

IIT Kanpur is reachable by Rail, Road and Air. You may choose to:

Take a train to Kanpur Central Railway Station (CNB). There are plenty of conveyance options available from the station to campus. We'll have a bus service at regular intervals (at ₹25) whose schedule will be intimated to you. If you don't want to wait for the next bus, you can take a Vikram or Auto to the campus (costs about ₹20 or ₹200 respectively). Or you can always go for an Ola Cab (at around ₹225-300).

Take a Bus ride to Kanpur. The conveyance from the Kanpur Bus Stand is again through a Vikram or an Auto (costs ₹20 or ₹200 respectively). Alternatively, you can call an Ola (costs around ₹225-300). Your own car is also welcome if you stay nearby!

Fly in to Lucknow International Airport (LKO) and take up a taxi to reach IITK Campus directly (A taxi costs around ₹1,550). Ofcourse, there are other hectic ways as well, but this is the fastest one!

Once you reach IIT Kanpur, the first thing you do is Report & Register at the Hospitality Desk. Here at the hospitality desk, you'll need the following for hassle-free registration:

Your College ID-card.

No Objection Certificate (NOC) issued by your college administration.

Proof of Registration fee payment if you have paid online (Hardcopy of the Receipt generated). You may choose to pay in Cash then and there as well.

₹200 in cash as Security Deposit, which will be refunded when you are leaving the campus.

Accommodation will be provided by us from the morning of 28th August 2015 till the afternoon of 31st August 2015, at par with that of IITK students. Participants arriving before that can stay in hotels outside IIT Kanpur. Mattresses will be provided to all participants, however you'll have to bring your own blankets.
